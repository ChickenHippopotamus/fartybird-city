#  FartyBird City Night Edition

A hilarious HTML5 remake of Flappy Bird where the bird flies by farting... through the city at night!

##  How to Play

1. Open index.html in any modern web browser
2. **Choose your character** from 8 unique birds!
3. Click **PLAY** to start
4. **Tap**, **Click**, or press **Space** to make the bird fart and fly!
5. Avoid the skyscrapers
6. Try to beat your high score!

##  Features

###  City Night Theme
- **Twinkling stars** and glowing **moon** in the night sky
- **City silhouette** in the background
- **Skyscrapers with lit windows** instead of pipes
- Rooftop details: antennas, water towers, pointed spires
- **City street** with animated lane markings and manholes

###  8 Playable Characters
1. **Cool Duck** - Yellow duck with cap, shades & leather jacket
2. **Beanie Bird** - Blue bird with beanie & gold chain
3. **Crying Bird** - Green bird laughing with tears
4. **Bandana Duck** - Yellow duck with pink bandana & shades
5. **Fancy Bird** - Red bird with blue dress & bow
6. **Derpy Bird** - Red bird with overalls & big derpy eyes
7. **Queen Bird** - Blue bird with backwards cap & QUEEN chain
8. **Chonky Bird** - Green chunky bird with tank top

###  Audio
- **Hip-hop inspired beat** - Looping city vibe background music
- **6 Random Fart Sound Types** - Tight, Dry, Rolling, Wet, Sticky, Long
- FM synthesis for realistic flatulence

###  Fart Clouds
- **Speckled particles** inside each cloud
- **6 color palettes** - Brown, green, purple, orange, blue, grey
- Randomized density and particle sizes

##  Technical Details

- Pure HTML5 Canvas
- Vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks)
- Web Audio API for synthesized sounds & music
- Responsive design
- LocalStorage for score persistence
- Single file - no external dependencies!

##  Fart Sound Types (Gastro-Synth 9000)

1. **The Squeak** - High pitch, short, squeaky
2. **The Ripper** - Classic sawtooth, fast drop
3. **The Motorboat** - Low pitch, heavy rattle modulation
4. **The Oops** - Wet, high resonance with bubbling
5. **The Sticky** - Tearing sound, square wave
6. **The Long Goodbye** - 2 second slow decay
